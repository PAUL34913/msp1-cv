16/12/2021 - My First Blog Post

Welcome to my blog page, and thank you for visiting my site!

It's been a very busy month since beginning my "Full Stack Web Development" course with Code Institute. A lot has been learned, and I can honestly say I have really enjoyed the course content so far, already I am almost at the end of my first milestone project.

The projects aim is to build a static webpage using HTML and CSS, and I have chosen to build an online resume as I believe it will be most useful to me at this stage.

Whilst you are here, if you have the time. Please listen to a song I wrote and recorded remotely with friends during the initial lockdown of the pandemic. The title of the song is "You" and was written collectively under the name of "Sick of Sirens". We currently perform under the name as a three-piece acoustic act in our local area, you can check out our Facebook page Here.
